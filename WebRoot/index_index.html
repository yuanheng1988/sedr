What is iTechs Software Data Repository?

    The central task of empirical software engineering (ESE) studies is the use of data to address a research question. The task of ESE researchers is to understand the nature of the process data, artifacts and their relationships. Thus, empirical data is indispensable for ESE studies to analyze, manipulate and draw conclusions from empirical studies. To establish the ground theory of ESE, we need to collect empirical data as much as possible and to make our experiments and case studies repeatable and comparable. With this idea, the ESE researchers in iTeches developed the Software Data Repository which may enable ESE researcher to

      store their data in uniform format in the Internet and retrieve the data in the future;

     share their data with the ESE researchers over the world;

     search, filter, merge and reuse the data in the repository on their research interest.

The functions in the Software Data Repository are provided as below.

      “Home”is the index page (i.e. current page).

      In “Browse Repository”, you may browse all the data in the repository.

      User may upload their data in “Share Data”

      Data may be previewed, filtered, merged and exported in “Export Data”.

      Users may present us with their comments, advices and questions in “Feedback & FAQ”.
